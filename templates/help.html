Help & Instructions

Getting Started

Welcome to RIQ Lab Matcher! This platform helps you find and connect with research opportunities at Harvard University.

Step 1: Create an Account

Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation bar to create your account. You'll need:

  • A valid email address
  • A secure password

Step 2: Browse Labs

Navigate to the General page to browse available Principal Investigators (PIs) and their research labs.

  • Use the filters to search by School (e.g., Harvard) and Department (e.g., Computer Science, Biology)
  • Click "Apply Filters" to see matching labs
  • Each lab card shows the PI's name, title, research areas, and contact information

Step 3: Save PIs

When you find labs that interest you:

  • Click the "Save PI" button on any lab card (you must be logged in)
  • View all your saved PIs on the Saved PIs page
  • You can remove saved PIs from your list at any time

Step 4: Upload Your Resume

Prepare for research applications by uploading your resume:

  • Go to the Resume Upload page
  • Upload a PDF or DOCX file
  • Your resume will be used for future AI-powered matching features

Step 5: Draft Emails

Use our AI-powered email drafting tool to write professional cold emails:

  • Navigate to Draft Email
  • Select a PI from your saved list or browse all PIs
  • Fill in your information and research interests
  • Click "Generate Email Draft" to create a personalized email
  • Copy the draft and send it via your email client

Tips for Success

  • Research First: Read about each PI's research areas before reaching out
  • Be Specific: Mention specific research projects or papers that interest you
  • Show Enthusiasm: Express genuine interest in their work
  • Be Professional: Use the email draft tool, but personalize it further
  • Follow Up: If you don't hear back, send a polite follow-up after 1-2 weeks

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a Harvard student?

No! While many labs are at Harvard, the platform is open to all students seeking research opportunities.

How do I know if a lab is accepting students?

Contact the PI directly using the email provided. Many labs welcome inquiries from motivated students.

Can I save multiple PIs?

Yes! Save as many PIs as you'd like. There's no limit to your saved list.

Is my resume data secure?

Yes, your uploaded resumes are stored securely and only used for matching purposes within the platform.

How accurate is the email draft tool?

The AI generates professional email drafts, but you should always review and personalize them before sending.

Need More Help?

If you have additional questions or encounter any issues, please contact the RIQ Lab Matcher support team.
